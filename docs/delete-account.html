TinySaver Kids — data deletion

You can permanently delete your TinySaver Kids account at any time. This erases your profile and all saved data — and it cannot be undone.

Option 1 · In-app (fastest)

If you still have the app installed:

  1. Open TinySaver Kids and sign in.
  2. Tap the BUDDY tab (bottom right).
  3. Enter your 4-digit parent PIN.
  4. Scroll to the bottom of the parent dashboard.
  5. Tap Delete my account and confirm with your password.

Everything is removed immediately.

Option 2 · By email

If you no longer have the app installed:

Email us at bicolallan@gmail.com

Please include the following so we can locate and remove your account:

  • The email address you signed up with
  • Your child's display name (as shown in the app)
  • Subject line: "Delete my TinySaver Kids account"

We will confirm receipt within 2 business days and permanently delete your account within 7 days.

What gets deleted

  • Your account and password
  • Child's display name and pig/mascot name
  • Coin balance and currency settings
  • All tasks you created and completion history
  • Reward goals (active, completed, redeemed)
  • Owned mascots and worn accessories
  • Daily streak and mission progress
  • Transaction history
  • Pro purchase flag (if applicable — refunds are handled separately by Google Play / App Store)

What we keep

Anonymized crash reports (no personal data) may remain in Firebase Crashlytics for up to 90 days before automatic purging. These are not linked to your identity and cannot be traced back to you.

Pro refunds

Deleting your account does not automatically refund a prior TinySaver Pro purchase. If you want a refund:

  • Android: request via Google Play order history within Google's refund window.
  • iOS: request via reportaproblem.apple.com.

Deletion is permanent

This cannot be undone. Once deleted, we cannot restore your coins, tasks, reward goals, or streak. Please make sure you want to proceed before confirming.

Questions

For any questions about deletion or your data, contact us at bicolallan@gmail.com.

See also our Privacy Policy for details on what data we collect and how it's stored.
